Privacy Policy

Extension Name: Social Media Ad Collector

Version: 0.6.0

Developer: Marcos Lerma /The University of Memphis

Purpose

This browser extension is designed for academic research purposes. It collects social media content to study advertisements and sponsored content on social media platforms.

Data Collection

This extension collects the following data for academic research purposes:

  • Social media post content: Text, images, and videos from social media posts
  • Screenshots: Compressed screenshots of media content (images and videos)
  • Participant identification: Prolific ID for linking participation data with compensation
  • Metadata: Timestamps, engagement metrics (likes, retweets, etc.), and post metadata
  • Detection data: Information about whether content is identified as advertisements, sponsored content, or contains specific indicators

Data Usage

Data is collected solely for academic research on advertisements and sponsored content in social media. The data will be analyzed to understand patterns in advertising content, user engagement, and the prevalence of advertisements on social media platforms.

Data will be used for:

  • Statistical analysis of advertisement patterns and trends
  • Academic publication and presentation
  • Understanding within-group and between-group differences in content exposure

Data Storage and Security

Data is securely stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud infrastructure:

  • Storage location: AWS S3 and DynamoDB (US East region)
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AWS encryption)
  • Access control: Data is only accessible to the research team
  • Backup: Data is stored redundantly on AWS infrastructure

Data Retention

Data will be retained for the duration of the research study and in accordance with institutional data retention policies. After the study is complete, data may be retained for:

  • Publication and peer review processes
  • Follow-up analyses
  • As required by institutional policies

Data will be securely deleted when no longer needed for research purposes, in accordance with institutional guidelines.

Data Sharing

Data is not shared with third parties. Data is only accessible to:

  • The principal investigator and research team
  • Institutional review board (IRB) members (for oversight purposes)
  • As required by law or institutional policy

Aggregated, anonymized results may be published in academic journals or presented at conferences, but individual participant data will not be identifiable.

Participant Rights

As a research participant, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw: You can withdraw from the study at any time by uninstalling the extension. Data collection stops immediately upon uninstallation.
  • Access: You can request information about what data has been collected about your participation (contact information below).
  • Questions: You can ask questions about the research, data collection, or your rights as a participant at any time.
